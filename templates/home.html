ABOUT US

Who are we?

Hi! eksochh is female powered and focused on Empowering women around the world. At eksochh we talk about our bodies, sexuality, sex-positive education, various cancers, self love, mental health and every single topic pertaining to females and the feminine experience.
We have started this organization to bring change by promoting reproductive and sex-positive education, awarness on various cancers and mental health. As we know, these topics are necessary nowadays. So we want your support for this organization by spreading awarness.
We are honest, badass and no topic is too taboo for us. We are a small community of college students from around the world. We hope you will join us in our mission.

OUR MISSION

Our Goal, Vision and Commitment

Uncensored

Women's bodies, voices, words and issues are highly censored. Women are scared to speak about issues like abuse,sex even their own reproductive rights. So we want to create a platform where women are fully free to say and do what they want without fear.

Unmuted

Women Should not have to be quiet to be respected! We want all women to know that they have a Voice and they should use it!

Unfiltered

Girls grow up whith so much pressure and unrealistic expectations of what their body should be like. There are some women who are already unfiltered no matter the company. You open yourself up to more progress, more fun, more laughs & more everything!

Support

We provide 24/7 help to all women who desperately need someone. If you need someone to talk to, we listen. We won't judge and tell you what to do.
